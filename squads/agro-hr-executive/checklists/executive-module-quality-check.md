# Executive Module Quality Check

## Strategic Quality
- [ ] The module starts from a business problem relevant to agribusiness executives
- [ ] Strategic HR is framed as capability, not administration
- [ ] At least 4 core frameworks are used with clear roles
- [ ] Workforce, leadership, and governance are all covered

## Pedagogical Quality
- [ ] The module fits 2-4 sessions coherently
- [ ] Every session has a hook, framework, case, and transfer prompt
- [ ] Slide outline is clear enough for instructor build-out
- [ ] Activities force executive judgment, not passive recall

## Agribusiness Relevance
- [ ] Seasonality or labor volatility appears where relevant
- [ ] Dispersed operations or frontline leadership appears where relevant
- [ ] Family business or governance issues appear where relevant
- [ ] Examples sound like real agribusiness settings

## Delivery Readiness
- [ ] There is one completed module template
- [ ] There is one session plan per session
- [ ] There is at least one agribusiness case lab
- [ ] The module ends with a 30-day executive action lens
